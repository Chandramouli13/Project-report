Project Name : Prime video

Prime Video is a streaming video service by Amazon. Prime Video benefits are included with an Amazon Prime membership. With your membership, you can watch hundreds of TV shows and movies on your favorite devices. To get started, visit Amazon.com/primevideo, or download the Prime Video app on your desktop/mobile device.
